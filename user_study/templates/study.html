Mobility Routing Study

Evaluating personalised multimodal route recommendations — Phase 1

Welcome!

Thank you very much for participating in our survey. It is being conducted as part of the ERDF-funded project "IMIQ – Smart Mobility in the Quarter".

"IMIQ – Intelligent Mobility Space in the Quarter" is a project of the IMR (Intelligent Mobility Space), which is based at the Wissenschaftshafen in Magdeburg. During its planned runtime from 2024 to 2028, the Wissenschaftshafen will become a district of the future where new needs-based solutions are conceived, technically tested, and implemented with a socio-economic perspective in mind.

In this study, we are interested in your opinions on personalised multimodal route recommendations. We would like to emphasise that there are no wrong answers — we are only interested in your personal assessment. This study is anonymous. Please answer all questions. It will take about 20–25 minutes to complete. Thank you again for your support!

Data Protection Information

Your consent to the processing of your data is voluntary. It may be modified or revoked in whole or in part at any time, effective for the future, without providing a reason. You may revoke your consent by mail or email using the contact information provided for the aforementioned contact person. Revoking consent will not result in any disadvantages for you.

Information on the collection of data in accordance with Art. 13 of the GDPR.
Your personal data will be processed for the following purpose: the survey data will be stored only in pseudonymised form and used for research purposes. Your data will be stored until the project is completed, or for a maximum period of 12 months. After that, all data will be deleted.

The legal basis for the collection of data is Article 6(1)(a) of the GDPR (consent). Data will not be disclosed to third parties (outside the university) or to a third country. Information regarding the responsible authority, the contact details of the data protection officers, and your rights regarding the processing of your personal data can be found in the OVGU's privacy policy at: https://www.ovgu.de/unimagdeburg/en/privacy+policy-p-64704.html

The data collected as part of this study will be used exclusively for scientific purposes. The results may be released in a scientific publication. This will be done in pseudonymised form, meaning that the data cannot be traced back to any specific individuals. To enhance the quality of research, the fully pseudonymised raw data from this study may be made available as open data on the internet in a data archive or provided to other researchers. In doing so, this study follows the recommendations of the German Research Foundation (DFG) and the German Psychological Society (DGPs) on quality control in research.
